Nutrition Fundamentals

Optimize your diet to support training, recovery, and long-term transformation.

🥗

Pre-Workout Fueling

Consume complex carbs and lean protein 2-3 hours before training. Examples: oatmeal with eggs, rice with chicken, sweet potato with lean beef.

🍎

Post-Workout Recovery

Protein and carbs within 30 minutes post-exercise for optimal muscle repair and glycogen replenishment. Protein shakes, Greek yogurt, or solid meals.

💧

Hydration Protocol

Drink half your body weight (in pounds) in ounces daily. Increase during intense training. Electrolyte supplements during long sessions.

📊

Macro Breakdown

50% carbs, 25% protein, 25% fats for muscle building. Adjust based on goals: cut at 40% carbs / 30% protein / 30% fats.

🌙

Sleep Nutrition

Casein protein before bed for sustained amino acid release. Avoid caffeine after 2 PM. Magnesium supplements for deeper sleep.

🏆

Supplement Stack

Creatine monohydrate, BCAAs, whey protein, multivitamin, fish oil. Consult coaches for personalized recommendations.
